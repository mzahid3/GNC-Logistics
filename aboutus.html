About Us

LoadPool, LLC is a full service Brokerage company serving customers in Transportation and Logistics industries by establishing a close partnership to serve their needs. Residing in Chicago, Illinois, LoadPool LLC offers a complete range of Brokerage services in all 50 states. Our services include Truckload, Less than Truckload (LTL), Domestic Air Freight, Ocean, Supply-Chain, Intermodal, Climate Controlled Warehousing, Pick and Pack Services, Dedicated Contract Cartage, Direct Mail Distribution Services, Hot Shot Services, and Local Cartage. As a non-asset based company, LoadPool LLC has access to equipment including Dry Vans, Temperature Controlled (refrigerated or frozen), Flat Beds, and Dump Trailers.

For construction industry specific, LoadPool LLC provides “On Demand” trucking services where customer can submit dirt/material pickup or delivery request online through our smart phone App. Our Company will pick up or deliver Construction loads from or to your site/location as soon as you submit the request. Our semi trucks are ready and available throughout the year to fulfill your load pickup or delivery request. Visit Apple or Android App stores to download LoadPool app.
